About MYS

Motified Youth Summit 2019 is planned to be an annual knowledge based activity concerning oriented leadership, entrepreneurship, good governance and democracy youth training program in Nigeria and Africa at large. Motified Youth Summit intends to bring together aspiring and careerdriven youth to deepen their leadership, entrepreneurship and democracy knowledge and foster active youth participation in good governance processes and democracy building within the region.

The summit is a convergence of young and aspiring entrepreneurs, academician, researcher, seasoned entrepreneurs, practitioners and government policy makers with the objectives of molding the orientation of participants towards entrepreneurship as well as serving as a platform to reorientate Nigerian Youths on Nigeria Economy and proffering a solution to the economic woes of the country.

Vision

To become the Nation's leading facilitator of opportunities that improve the lives and livelihood of the Nation's Youth by Creating a hub of relevant Knowledge that will empower and prepare them for a better future.

Mission

We are professionally committed to building a new generation of responsible Nigeria Youths and worth Role Models who will make impact in the country and beyond.

Goals and Objectives

  • To create a Platform to help create, share and develop and implement new ideas to help actualize dreams
  • To empower youth into becoming proactive in breaking gender inequality and actualizing their dreams
  • To provide educational leadership opportunities that will encourage them to achieve their full potentials.

Motified Youth Summit Roundtable 09:00AM - 10:00AM Motified Youth Summit Career/Talent Discovery 10:00AM - 11:00AM Networking/Break 11:00AM - 12:00PM Motified Youth Summit (INTERVIEW SESSION) 12:00PM - 01:00PM MOTIFIED AWARD 2019 01:00PM - 02:00PM LAUNCHING MOTIFIED FOUNDATION 'STREET OUTREACH ' 02:00PM - 03:00PM

This section of the MYS 2019 will encompass the Economy and Political Issue of the Nation. These youth roundtable discussions will allow youth from around the world to sit together and discuss current Economic and Political issues affecting the nation and suggest what policies they feel should be implemented to address these issues. In order to ensure that these discussions are noticed, politicians and Successful Business men will be invited to sit in on these discussions and hear what the youth have to say. The results of these discussions will be presented in a report format after the close of MYS 2019 and be publicly available on the MYS website . In order to prepare for these discussions and to give the youth a starting point for their discussions, a series of topics and issues will be convened (through internet web forums) prior to the meetings to produce documents presenting various youth opinions surrounding these topics. These documents will also help youth prepare for the roundtable discussions before arriving at MYS2019. These documents will also enable the reporting of roundtable results to be expedited, since they can form the beginning of the final report. This final report will serve as the medium for MYS to communicate the results of the roundtable discussions to politicians, policy and decision makers, Business men, the general public and youth from around the world.
